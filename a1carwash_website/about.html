Our company

A1 Car Wash is a family owned and run business. Our mission is to provide our customers with the highest quality car wash available in the quickest, most efficient period of time. We use a unique brushless system and a small amount of equipment to wash your car while maintaining a high degree of quality. Whether you choose the fast lane or a complete detail, we make sure to get you in and out quickly–with spectacular results.

At A1 Car Wash we know how good it feels to drive a clean car. Whether you visit one of our full Service detailing (interior and exterior) or exterior, express car washes, you can expect the same great experience. We guarantee you the best car wash experience, from our professional, attentive customer service advisors and clean facilities to our complimentary fresh coffee & popcorn and wi-fi.

Today our carwashes feature the most advanced technology available, specially formulated detergents, soft cloth, computer-controlled equipment and our special touchless hot air dryers, along with the exceptional customer service for which A1 is known for. Our dedicated and talented team members work hard to earn your trust and keep you coming back. We know that next to your home, your vehicle is likely the second largest investment you’ll make and caring for it safely is a privilege we don’t take lightly.
