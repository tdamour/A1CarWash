Helping the community!

A1 Car Wash donates $25K to the states Environmental fund.

October 9, 2011 (Albuquerque, NM) A1 Car Wash representative, Skyler, presented a check in the amount of $25,000 to the New Mexico's Environmental fund to help clean up the states ground water.

A1's "Love Chemistry" program benefits local high schools.

September 15, 2012 (Albuquerque, NM) A1 has always been dedicated to improving the lives of youth through its "Love Chemistry" outreach program. Annually, more than 2,000 high school kids from across the state benefit from the contributions that A1 gives to help promote interest in chemistry.

A1 Carwash to donate over $10,000 to Big Brothers / Big Sisters.

February 26, 2013 (Albuquerque, NM) Thanks to the overwhelming generosity of the local community, A1 Car Wash will donate $10,400 to Big Brothers/Big Sisters of the Rio Rancho Valley to support local one-to-one youth mentoring and educational programming. The money was raised during a grand opening charity weekend when customers were given a free external car washes. In lieu of payment for the carwash, customers could choose to make a donation to support this organizations. “We are so pleased that so many people came out and gave generously during this event. The money will go directly to support local mentoring programs that start success stories in our community,” says County Sherrif, Bogdan Wolynetz.
